Kenya

Kenya is a country in East Africa. It is known for its stunning landscapes, wildlife, and diverse cultures. The capital city, Nairobi, is a vibrant metropolis that is home to both bustling markets and tranquil nature reserves. Kenya's wildlife is world-renowned, with national parks like the Maasai Mara and Amboseli attracting tourists from around the world.
